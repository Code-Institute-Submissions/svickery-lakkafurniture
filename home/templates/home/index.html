About Lakka Furniture


Who Are We?

Established in the late 90's Lakka Furniture was the brain child of two friends - Sam and Simon - who wanted to make a change in the boring flat pack culture of furniture. They decided bespoke one of a kind items was the way to go! Their business quickly took off and realised that they couldn't keep up with the requests for their goods. Sam and Simon decided instead to mass produce some of their best items to keep customers happy. 20 years later and they are still doing it!


What Do We Do?

At Lakka we can offer you not only the best stock products but also one of a kind furniture! Please contact us for any bespoke items that you would like to have made specifically for you and your vision! Our contact details are below in the footer.
